About CineArtistry

The world's most elegant movie recommendation engine.

Our Mission

CineArtistry was born out of a passion for pure cinema. Our goal is to break the endless scrolling fatigue of modern streaming apps by providing a highly stylized, curated, and premium interface to discover gems across 5 native languages.

The Tech Stack

Powered by Python Flask, SQLite, Vanilla CSS Glassmorphism, and dynamic background syncs with public film databases (like OMDB) and YouTube to pull high-definition original artwork and trailers natively into our experience.
